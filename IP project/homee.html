Well Come to Cafeteria Management System

In Wolkite University cafeteria management system is one of the organization systems which provide food for university students and employees. It is belongs to under the collage of university. We collect the information about the system through interview, observation and document analysis. This system for giving food for the student and giving salary for its employee.
